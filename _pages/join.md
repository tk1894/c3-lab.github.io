# Work with Us


## Students


**We are actively recruiting** students who are excited about culture‑centered computing and care.


Good fits often include:
- Curiosity about community‑engaged research
- Comfort learning new tools independently
- Interest in ethical
- XAI
- Programming (Python, C++ ...)


**How to reach out**
- Email a brief introduction + your goals
- Include your CV/resume
- Share any relevant portfolio links


## Community Partners


If you’re a community organization, clinic, educator, or local/Tribal partner, we’d love to explore collaboration on: research, grant proposal, community activities...
- Co‑designing tools to support care and wellness
- Running participatory workshops
- Evaluating technology in real contexts
